ICW Arena, Philadelphia, PA

Backstage, one hour before showtime, Shirlea Frost can be found confronting Johnny Sorrow over ICW's drug testing policy.

Shirlea: Come on, Johnny. Can't you reschedule my drug test? My father is really sick and I have to leave for Texas as soon as the show is over.

Johnny: I'm sorry to hear about your father, Shirlea, but the fact remains that you have to be tested. I can assure you that it will take less than an hour.

Shirlea trails her fingertip up Johnny's chest to his chin as she gives him a seductive smile.

Shirlea: I can make it worth your while, Johnny.

Johnny: Besides the fact that I’m old enough to be your father, I’m also very happily married. Now if you will excuse me, I have to prepare for the show.

As Johnny walks off, Shirlea stomps her foot in anger.

Shirlea: You old bastard! You couldn’t handle me even with a truckload of Viagra.

A laugh can be heard as Bruce Vain walks up behind Shirlea.

Bruce: Don't tell me. You're worried about a little thing like a drug test?

Shirlea: Why don't you fuck off?

Bruce: Is that an invitation?

Voice: I don't think the lady wants any part of you.

Bruce: I don't care what you think.

Suddenly, Scotty Paine slams Bruce into the wall and rears back his fist.

Bruce: Not the face! Not the face!

Scotty laughs as he releases Bruce, who scurries away like a scolded dog.

Scotty: Well, I think that deserves a reward. What do to think, baby?

Shirlea slaps Scotty across the face and turns, walking away in a huff. Scotty raises his hand to the red spot on his face and smiles.

Scotty: She’s feisty. I like it.

At that moment, Jonathan Black walks up and stands face to face with Scotty.

John: Stay away from her, Paine.

Scotty: Or what?

John: If you value your job, don't cross me.

The two men stand there silently staring one another down until Scotty turns and walks away laughing loudly.
