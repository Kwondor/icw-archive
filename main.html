News & Updates
November 28, 2009: The first ever Saturday Night Shock premiered with a big bang! We saw the ICW début of a second generation superstar, a triple threat match that was all action from bell to bell, and a main event that saw "The Legend By Design," John Patrick, take on the man from the Land of the Rising Sun, Shinya Okubo. All of this, plus a huge birthday bash for ICW co-founder LJ Black! Click here to read the results!

November 21, 2009: ICW's first card has been announced! Saturday Night Shock will be held on November 28th, and will be the first of three shows to be hailing from ICW Arena in Philadelphia. Click here to view the lineup!

November 7, 2009: LJ and Trinity Black are proud to announce the opening of Intercontinental Championship Wrestling. At this time, it can be revealed the names of the first three wrestlers to sign a contract with the company: Leslie Case-Inman, her twin sister Kelsey Case, and Scotty Paine. On behalf of ICW management, we wish the three of them the very best during their stay in ICW.
